Prototyping

From creating physics simulations, to experimental AR samples for promotion, here are some brief breakdowns of developmental projects I have worked on.

Augmented Reality Product Demos

I have worked with multiple Augmented reality web tools, one of which was used as a tech demonstration for my role as a VR Game Developer. Made using MyWebAR, I created a promotional QR code that when scanned, showed some assets from the game displayed above the QR code. This was displayed at the front desk of the boxing gym the game was being developed at.

Alongside MyWebAR, I have used other AR tools, such as AR.js and Three.js to create similar scenes, without the need for a QR code.

Arduino And Raspberry Pi

Software Developer

As a student, I took a course about Digital logic systems I gained a solid understanding of object-oriented programming using C++. I applied this knowledge to create physics simulations and utilized a graphics library to visualize my work. Additionally, I experimented with programming Arduino Uno and Raspberry Pi devices. For my final project, I developed a GPS device with an interactive display for geocaching, which integrated with Google Maps to showcase the locations visited by the user.
